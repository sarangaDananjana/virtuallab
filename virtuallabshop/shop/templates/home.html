Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How do you handle very large games (100GB+)?

We professionally split large games across multiple DVDs (up to 10+ discs for massive titles). Each disc is clearly labeled with the installation order, and we include detailed step-by-step instructions to make the process smooth.

Are the games safe to install?

Yes, all our game installers are thoroughly checked and verified before burning to DVDs. We've been serving the Sri Lankan gaming community for over 5 years with thousands of satisfied customers. If you encounter any issues, our support team is always ready to help.

What platforms do you support?

We exclusively focus on PC (Windows) games to provide the best possible service. Our games are optimized for Windows 10 and Windows 11 systems.

How fast is delivery?

We offer fast delivery across Sri Lanka, typically within 24-48 hours for most locations. Express delivery options are available for Colombo and suburbs. Exact delivery times will be confirmed when you place your order.

Do you provide installation support?

Absolutely! We're known for our excellent after-service support. If you face any installation issues, simply open a support ticket and our team will guide you through the process within 24 hours. We're here to ensure you can enjoy your games hassle-free.

Can I request specific games?

Yes! If you don't see a game in our catalog, you can request it through our support system. We'll check availability and prepare it for you, usually within 2-3 business days.
