Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Which format should I choose?

Choose DVDs if you're a collector, have limited budget, or prefer physical media. Choose Hard Disks if you want multiple games at once, faster installation, or plan to order frequently. Both formats include our full support and installation assistance.

How fast is delivery?

We offer fast delivery across Sri Lanka, typically within 24-48 hours for most locations. Express delivery options are available for Colombo and suburbs. Exact delivery times will be confirmed when you place your order.

Do you provide installation support?

Absolutely! We're known for our excellent after-service support. If you face any installation issues, simply open a support ticket and our team will guide you through the process within 24 hours. We're here to ensure you can enjoy your games hassle-free.

Can I request specific games?

Yes! If you don't see a game in our catalog, you can request it through our support system. We'll check availability and prepare it for you in your preferred format (DVD or Hard Disk), usually within 2-3 business days.
